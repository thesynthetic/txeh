What is a hext link?

Hext.me is a site where you can create memorable links using real words and phrases. Simply think of a catchphrase, assign a link to it, and voila! You have created a hextlink. Now every time somebody types in your phrase, it will link him/her directly to the page of your choosing. Use our “dashboard” feature to view how many people are checking out your link, who they are, and where they’re from.

How do I create a hextlink?

Unleash the power of hextlinks in just two steps: 1. Input your own new memorable catchphrase 2. Input the URL of the page you want to link to Just like that, you’ve created your very own unique hextlink. You can change the linking URL at anytime, and you can also view statistics of how many people checked out your link, who they are, and where they’re from.
